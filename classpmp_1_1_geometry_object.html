Garbage Collection

Garbage collection has been split into three methods:

  • garbageCollection (initiating the process)
  • beginGarbage (overridable, remove elements) and
  • finalizeGarbage (overridable, resize vectors)

This makes extending the mechanism easier for subclasses. Subclasses can access the count of initial vertices and the count of remaining vertices after the cleanup at the same time (during beginGarbage) and access the according vertex properties. Then in finalizeGarbage, these vectors are resized accordingly.
